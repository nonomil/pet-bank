# 飞机大战首页卡图短提示词

适合直接粘贴到网页版 ChatGPT / Agnes。

## 推荐先试

### 版本 A1

Create a bright sci-fi arcade illustration for a children's typing shooter game. A small heroic spaceship on the left fires a glowing laser to the right. Three to four enemy drones on the right are flying toward the player. One enemy is being hit with a bright energy flash and a small explosion. Make the scene exciting, clear, and easy to understand at a glance. Bright teal, cyan, lime glow, and warm orange highlights. Slightly toy-like but still cool, not babyish, not dark, not military realistic. Leave a few blank floating panel shapes near the top for future UI overlay. No text, no letters, no numbers, no Chinese, no fake writing, no watermark.

## 负面修正词

如果生成结果偏暗、偏乱、偏成熟，可以补一句：

`Make it brighter, cleaner, more playful, more readable, and more appealing to a young child.`

如果生成结果太幼稚、太卡通，可以补一句：

`Keep it cool and sleek, not babyish, not chibi, not overly cute.`

如果没有打中的爽感，可以补一句：

`Show a strong visible laser hit and a satisfying impact flash.`

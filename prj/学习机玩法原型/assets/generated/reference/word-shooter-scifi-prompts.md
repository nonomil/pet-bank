# Word Shooter sci-fi refresh prompts

These prompts replace the earlier cute toy-like direction for `word-shooter`.

Target mood: closer to a clean neon sci-fi typing arcade like the user's `Typing Attack` reference, but still suitable for a children's learning prototype. No horror, no war damage, no blood, no realistic violence, no readable text baked into images.

Project rule: all English words, Chinese hints, score text, combo text, and UI labels remain HTML/CSS, not image text.

## Full reference

Create one polished 16:9 landscape reference image for a sci-fi English typing shooter web game for children ages 6 to 8.

Core scene:
- a small sleek training interceptor ship or compact defense drone sits on the left side, facing right
- multiple word targets drift in from the right side toward the left, with one current target near the middle-right and two or three upcoming targets behind it
- every target has a blank central plate or hollow panel where HTML words can be overlaid later
- thin bright white-blue laser fire travels from the ship toward the current target
- the whole layout should feel like a typing attack training simulator, not a cute toy scene

Visual style:
- cool neon sci-fi arcade
- dark teal to navy background
- subtle technical grid or holographic training field
- cyan, white, electric blue, amber, and a little orange as accent colors
- crisp but stylized 2.5D game UI
- medium-small ship silhouette, not oversized
- targets should feel like drones, capsules, tech balloons, shield pods, cargo pods, hologram crates, or floating sci-fi plates
- cleaner shapes, less rounded baby-toy look, less pastel, less chibi

Composition:
- keep the left ship relatively small
- keep large open center space for moving words and attack lines
- leave space for a top HUD strip, a small Chinese hint card, a weapon status badge, and a combo panel
- show one weapon pickup floating after an exploded target
- show one target being hit with a brighter impact ring

Hard constraints:
- no readable text
- no letters
- no numbers
- no pinyin
- no Chinese characters
- no mascots
- no animal pilot
- no giant cute face
- no soft clay toy style
- no Minecraft branding
- no realistic military insignia
- no dark horror tone
